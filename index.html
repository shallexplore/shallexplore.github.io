About

If you've scrolled past his work, you've probably seen it more than once, just never known it was him. In marketing since 2017. Since then: a travel and wildlife channel, several faceless channels built on AI edits, a book, a quiz. Different formats, one instinct: find what people are missing and hand it back in a way they'll actually watch.
